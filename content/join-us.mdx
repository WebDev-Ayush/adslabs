Are you passionate about technology, development, and making a real impact? ADS Labs is the perfect platform for you to advance your skills, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and contribute to cutting-edge projects. Whether you're a coder, a designer, or a problem-solver, we’re looking for talented individuals to join our dynamic team.

**Why Join ADS Labs?**

At ADS Labs, we believe in pushing boundaries, solving complex problems, and creating solutions that matter. As part of our team, you'll have the opportunity to work on innovative projects, develop your skill set, and collaborate with some of the brightest minds in the field. Our mission is simple: **Advance. Develop. Succeed.**

**How to Apply**

Ready to take the next step? Joining ADS Labs is straightforward. Here’s what we need from you:

1. **Craft Your Application Email**
   - Write an email to **devxoshakya@gmail.com** with the subject line “ADS Labs Application.”
   - Include the following details:
     - **Your Contributions:** Share any major contributions you’ve made to projects, open-source communities, or anything else that showcases your skills.
     - **Skill Set:** List the technologies, tools, and languages you are proficient in.
     - **Specialty:** Highlight your area of expertise (e.g., Full-Stack Development, Machine Learning, Graphic Design).
     - **College and Branch:** Let us know your current academic status, including your college, branch, and year of study.

2. **Direct Entry for DSA Enthusiasts**
   - If you have a solid understanding of Data Structures and Algorithms (DSA), you’re in luck! Applicants with a **LeetCode profile showcasing 100+ solved questions** will get direct entry into ADS Labs. Just include a link to your LeetCode profile in your application.

3. **Requirements for Graphic Designers and Frontend Developers**
   - Are you a Graphic Designer or Frontend Developer? We’d love to see your work. Please include a link to your **portfolio or a project you’ve worked on** that highlights your design or frontend development skills.
